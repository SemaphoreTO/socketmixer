Trim Line Smoothing Process

In the following steps, you will attach a mounting point to the distal end of the socket.

Select Area

Select the top of the trim line by double-clicking on the flat, top surface of the socket. Use the ">" key on your keyboard to expand the area selected. Selecting a portion of the socket wall will allow for a smoother transition between the top of the socket and the trimline."

Smooth

Now, use the slider to adjust the smoothing of the trimline. Click "Accept" to smooth

Finished

Trim Line Smoothing


Your socket is now complete. Save a .mix file by going to the "File" menu and selecting "Save As." Once you have saved your model, export it as a .obj by clicking "Export" in the "File" menu. Make sure you select "OBJ Format" as the file type.
