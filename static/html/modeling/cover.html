Modeling


This section will take between 10 and 30 minutes.

To start, click the button on the black bar. The button will move you back one step. The button will move you to the end of this section. The button will bring you back to the start of the section. Additional and buttons appear in individual steps (on a grey background, inside task boxes), allowing you to move between smaller tasks.

This section will cover the following:
  • Importing your scan file
  • Removing excess data from the file
  • Rectifying the positive model
  • Creating a socket from the rectified positive
  • Adding an ICRC-compatible mounting point to the socket
