Modeling


This section will take between 10 and 30 minutes.

To start, click the button on the black bar. The button will move you back one step. The button will move you to the end of this section. The button will bring you back to the start of the section. Additional and buttons appear in individual steps (on a grey background, inside task boxes), allowing you to move between smaller tasks.

In Meshmixer, holding the "Space" bar opens a set of controls which change the way your models are displayed. Most importantly, the buttons in the "Color" section allow you to switch between displaying the original color data from your 3D scan, and displaying what are called "face groups." To toggle between the two views in Meshmixer, simply hold down the "Space" bar and click on either of the two icons in the "Color" area of the pop-up menu.
